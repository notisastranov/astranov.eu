Astranov Global Internet Operating System

Astranov is a web-based global OS: an interactive Earth globe, city street maps, local vendor discovery, and a delivery quote engine (distance, weight, weather, platform fee).

City maps & delivery

Open a city map from the globe, place or view nearby vendors, and compute delivery quotes with base fee, km blocks, kg blocks, night/weather surcharges, and 3% platform fee. Drivers receive payout on delivery.

How to use

  • Drag the Earth gently to rotate; scroll to zoom.
  • Use Locate for GPS, or City to open a street map.
  • Quote delivery with CLI: quote <km> [kg] [subtotal].
